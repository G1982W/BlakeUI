## Introduction

**Config Members 4** is a table-based team management UI:

- **Toolbar**: Search input, role filter popover (Admin, Editor, Viewer checkboxes), and Invite button.
- **Invite dialog**: Textarea for bulk email addresses and role selector.
- **Table**: Columns for Name, Email, Role, and Status. Status uses badges (e.g. Active, Pending).

Uses [Button](/docs/components/button), [Input](/docs/components/input), [Textarea](/docs/components/textarea), [Badge](/docs/components/badge), [Checkbox](/docs/components/checkbox), [Popover](/docs/components/popover), [Dialog](/docs/components/dialog), [Native Select](/docs/components/native-select), and [Table](/docs/components/table). Suited to settings pages that need search, filtering, and bulk invite.
