## Introduction

**Charts Group 1** is a business analytics dashboard:

- **Header**: Date range with navigation arrows, compare toggle, and export dropdown.
- **Stats row**: Key metrics (revenue, profit, orders).
- **Main chart**: Tabs to switch between revenue, profit, and orders visualizations.
- **Device breakdown**: Donut chart with center text.
- **Bento grid**: Responsive layout with multiple chart types.

Uses Chart, Tabs, Button, DropdownMenu, Popover, Calendar, and Recharts.
