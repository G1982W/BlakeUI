## Introduction

**Config Members 3** is a list-based members view:

- **Header**: “Members” title, subheading with a “Learn more” help link, search input, and Invite button.
- **List**: Each row shows avatar, name, email, role badge, and a three-dot menu (Change role, Remove). Invite dialog includes email and role selector.

Uses [Button](/docs/components/button), [Input](/docs/components/input), [Avatar](/docs/components/avatar), [Badge](/docs/components/badge), [Dropdown Menu](/docs/components/dropdown-menu), [Dialog](/docs/components/dialog), and [Native Select](/docs/components/native-select). Compact and suitable for smaller teams or when a table is not needed.
