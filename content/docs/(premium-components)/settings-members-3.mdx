## Introduction

**Config Members 1** displays team members in a card grid:

- **Header**: “Team members” title and Invite button that opens a dialog (email input, role selector).
- **Cards**: Each card shows avatar, name, email, color-coded role badge (Admin, Member, Viewer), join date, and a three-dot menu (Send email, Change role, Remove).

Uses [Button](/docs/components/button), [Input](/docs/components/input), [Avatar](/docs/components/avatar), [Badge](/docs/components/badge), [Dropdown Menu](/docs/components/dropdown-menu), [Dialog](/docs/components/dialog), and [Native Select](/docs/components/native-select). Suited to team/workspace settings where a visual, scannable layout is preferred.
